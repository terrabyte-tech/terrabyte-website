All About Pepperridge Farms!

Pepperridge Farms was founded in 1937, in Fairfield, Conneticut. Our founder was Margaret Rudkin who was an entreprenurial mom. She founded Pepperidge farms, by baking fresh, all natural breadfor her allergy-afflicted son. How we got our name was the property she lived on being named Pepperidge. It was named that because the property had an ancient Pepperidge tree growing there!
