Our Mission Statement

My brother Vincent Valenzuela built roots in Wakefield. After his passing, I moved to Wakefield to be here for his wife and kids. The community welcomed me and my girls as if we were already family. I saw and felt why my brother established his roots in Wakefield. Vincent (Vinny) left a powerful permanent footprint in the community. Vinny’s Cafe was built in his name and memory. He treated everyone like family, when guests walk into Vinny’s Cafe they are treated like family.
